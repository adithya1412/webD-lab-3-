Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • slices of bread
  • slices of cheddar cheese
  • slices of mozzarella cheese
  • Slices of bacon
  • tomato, sliced
  • one tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Cook bacon in a pan until crispy.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add cheddar and mozzarella cheese to the bread.
  5. Add bacon and tomato slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter side up.
  7. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
